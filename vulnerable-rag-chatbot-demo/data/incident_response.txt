TITLE: Public Incident Response Guide
CLASSIFICATION: public

Employees should report suspected phishing, malware, lost devices, or unusual login prompts to security@example.local.
For urgent incidents, include the affected system, time observed, screenshots if available, and whether customer data may be involved.
Do not forward suspicious attachments. Preserve the message and use the report phishing button when available.
Security will triage reports by severity and may ask the user to disconnect from VPN during containment.
